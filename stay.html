Suites

Ufulu Gardens offers spacious, recently refurbished hotel suites. Straying from the traditional “long corridor” passageway leading to the rooms, Ufulu Gardens has individual outside access to each suite, ensuring privacy and minimal run-ins with other guests.

Apartments

Each luxurious and spacious corporate apartment at Ufulu Gardens offers separate bedrooms and baths, kitchens and dining rooms, and all are fully-furnished with modern décor, full kitchen and dining amenities, telephone and WI-FI access. Enjoy the majestic scenery, tranquility, and flora and fauna of Ufulu Gardens right from your own private patio/veranda, or choose to stay in and view DSTV on the available 40-inch flat-screen television. All corporate apartments are fully attended to by our dedicated housekeeping staff daily, so you have more flexibility in your schedule for work or leisure.
